History

TechCon started in 2010 as a small meetup for local tech enthusiasts and has since evolved into an international conference drawing thousands of participants annually. Over the years, it has grown into a hub for innovators, developers, and entrepreneurs to share their vision of the future.

Mission

The mission of TechCon 2024 is to inspire and connect forward-thinking minds in the tech industry. We aim to drive innovation, encourage lifelong learning, and foster a community built on collaboration and exploration.

Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has welcomed some of the most influential voices in technology.

Izzet

AI Researcher and Professor at MIT, Dr. Izzet spoke in 2021 about the ethical future of artificial intelligence.

Elijah Tan

Founder of CodeFlow, Elijah delivered an inspiring keynote in 2022 on open-source leadership and community building.
